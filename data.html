Data Deletion Request

At Tappy Games, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. If you wish to request the deletion of your data associated with our games and services, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request the deletion of your data, please send an email to our support team at:

support@tappygames.com

In your email, please include the following information to help us process your request efficiently:

  • Game(s) Played: Specify which Tappy Games title(s) your request pertains to.
  • Account Identifier: If applicable, provide your in-game username, user ID, or the email address associated with your game account.
  • Reason for Deletion (Optional): While not required, providing a reason can help us improve our services.

What Happens Next

Once we receive your request, we will:

  • Acknowledge receipt of your request within 72 hours.
  • Verify your identity to ensure the security of your data. We may ask for additional information if necessary.
  • Process your data deletion request in accordance with applicable laws and our internal policies. This typically takes up to 30 days.
  • Confirm once your data has been deleted. Please note that some anonymized or aggregated data may be retained for analytical purposes, and some data may be kept if required by law.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about the data deletion process or our privacy practices, please refer to our Privacy Policy or contact us at privacy@tappygames.com.
