System Requirements

  • iOS 14.0 or later
  • Compatible with iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch
  • Requires an active internet connection for online features

Common Issues & Solutions

Game Won't Start or Crashes

  • Ensure your iOS version is up to date
  • Close all background apps and restart the game
  • Delete and reinstall the app
  • Ensure you have enough storage space (minimum 200MB recommended)

Save Progress Issues

  • Verify you're connected to the internet
  • Sign in to Game Center
  • Force close and restart the app

In-App Purchases

  • Ensure you're signed in to your Apple ID
  • Check your payment method is valid
  • Verify parental controls aren't restricting purchases
  • For missing purchases, use the "Restore Purchases" option in the game settings
